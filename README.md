## 🧐 About Me (The Hybrid Mode)

I operate at the intersection of two worlds that I am determined to merge:

* **The Researcher (PhD):** My work focuses on **Neuro-Symbolic AI**. I’m on a mission to marry the statistical power of Deep Learning with the explainable rigor of Symbolic Reasoning. Basically, I want AI to stop just *predicting* and start *reasoning*.
* **The Consultant (@ CGI):** I help organizations navigate the AI hype. I turn "Generative AI" buzzwords into robust, scalable, and—most importantly—reliable industrial solutions.

> **My Philosophy:** If a model needs 10 billion parameters to realize a cat isn't a dog, it might be missing a bit of common sense (and a few logical rules).
